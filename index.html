Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Is it necessary to have an account to shop on Spectrails?

Yes, it's necessary to log into your spectrails account to shop. Shopping as a logged-in user is fast & convenient and also provides extra security. You'll have access to a personalised shopping experience including recommendations and quicker check-out.

Why do I see different prices for the same product?

You could see different prices for the same product, as it could be listed by many Sellers.

How are items packaged?

Products sold by Sellers with spectrails Assured badge are shipped in packages with waterproof plastic wrap. Other Sellers also follow standard packing procedure. Sellers are rated on packaging quality and affects overall seller rating.

Are there any hidden charges (Octroi or Sales Tax) when I shop on spectrails ?

There are NO hidden charges when you shop on spectrails. The price you see on the product page is final and it's exactly what you pay. Note: There can be additional delivery charges based on the seller's policy.

What is spectrails Buyer Protection?

Spectrails Buyer Protection helps shoppers in case they have concerns with product(s) bought and haven't got a satisfactory solution from the seller. Under this program, we look into your concern on a case-by-case basis & do our best to be fair to both parties. Spectrails Buyer Protection decision on the resolution will be final for the concern raised.
